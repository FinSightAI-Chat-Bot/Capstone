<p style="Background-color:#FFFFFF;margin-top:12pt;margin-bottom:0pt;text-indent:7.14%;color:#000000;font-size:11pt;font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:normal;font-style:normal;text-transform:none;font-variant: normal;">In connection with these offerings, the legal opinion as to the legality of the Common Stock and the Notes sold is being filed as Exhibit 5.1 to this Current Report on Form&#160;8-K&#160;and is incorporated herein and into the Registration Statement by reference.</p>
<p style="Background-color:#FFFFFF;margin-top:18pt;margin-bottom:0pt;text-indent:0%;font-style:italic;color:#000000;font-size:11pt;font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:normal;text-transform:none;font-variant: normal;">Supplemental Indenture Relating to Convertible Senior Notes</p>
<p style="Background-color:#FFFFFF;margin-top:6pt;margin-bottom:0pt;text-indent:7.14%;color:#000000;font-size:11pt;font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:normal;font-style:normal;text-transform:none;font-variant: normal;">On May 7, 2019, the Company entered into a Fifth Supplemental Indenture relating to the issuance by the Company of the Notes (the &#8220;<font style="text-decoration:underline;">Supplemental Indenture</font>&#8221;) supplementing the Indenture, dated May&#160;22, 2013 (the &#8220;<font style="text-decoration:underline;">Base Indenture</font>,&#8221; and together with the Supplemental Indenture, the &#8220;<font style="text-decoration:underline;">Indenture</font>&#8221;), by and between the Company and U.S. Bank National Association , as trustee (the &#8220;<font style="text-decoration:underline;">Trustee</font>&#8221;). The Notes will bear interest at a rate of 2.00%&#160;per year, payable semi-annually in arrears on May&#160;15 and November&#160;15 of each year, commencing November&#160;15, 2019. The Notes will mature on May&#160;15, 2024, unless, in each case, earlier repurchased by the Company or converted pursuant to their respective terms.</p>
<p style="Background-color:#FFFFFF;margin-top:12pt;margin-bottom:0pt;text-indent:7.14%;color:#000000;font-size:11pt;font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:normal;font-style:normal;text-transform:none;font-variant: normal;">The initial conversion rate of the Notes is 3.2276 shares of Common Stock per $1,000 principal amount of the Notes (which is equivalent to an initial conversion price of approximately $309.83 per share). The conversion rate for the Notes will be subject to adjustment upon the occurrence of certain specified events but will not be adjusted for accrued and unpaid interest. In addition, upon the occurrence of a &#8220;make-whole fundamental change&#8221; (as defined in the Supplemental Indenture), the Company will, in certain circumstances, increase the conversion rate by a number of additional shares for a holder that elects to convert the Notes in connection with such make-whole fundamental change.</p>

(2)&#160;during the five business day period after any fi</font><font style="color:#000000;font-size:11pt;font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:normal;font-style:normal;text-transform:none;font-variant: normal;">ve consecutive trading day period in which the trading price per $1,000 principal amount of the Notes for each trading day of such period was less than 98% of the product of the last reported sale price of Common Stock and the conversion rate on each such </font><font style="color:#000000;font-size:11pt;font-family:Times New Roman;font-weight:normal;font-style:normal;text-transform:none;font-variant: normal;">trading day;
