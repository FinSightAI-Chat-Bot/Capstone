<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><B>Other Events </B></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On March&nbsp;27, 2017, Goldman, Sachs&nbsp;&amp; Co., Deutsche Bank
Securities Inc., Citigroup Global Markets Inc. and Morgan Stanley&nbsp;&amp; Co. LLC, as representatives of the underwriters (the &#147;<U>Underwriters</U>&#148;) of Tesla, Inc.&#146;s (the &#147;<U>Company</U>&#148;) previously consummated public
offering of its 2.375% Convertible Senior Notes due March&nbsp;15, 2022 (the &#147;<U>Notes</U>&#148;), notified the Company of the Underwriters&#146; exercise in full of their option to purchase an additional $127.5&nbsp;million aggregate principal
amount of the Notes (the &#147;<U>Option Notes</U>&#148;). The Option Notes were sold pursuant to a Registration Statement on Form <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">S-3</FONT> (File <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">No.&nbsp;333-211437)</FONT> (the
&#147;<U>Registration Statement</U>&#148;) and a related prospectus, including the related prospectus supplement, filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission. On March&nbsp;30, 2017, the Company issued the Option Notes. The Option Notes were
issued pursuant to a supplemental indenture, dated as of March 22, 2017 (the &#147;<U>Fourth Supplemental Indenture</U>&#148;), supplementing the indenture, dated as of May 22, 2013, by and between the Company and U.S. Bank National Association, as
trustee. A description of the terms of the Fourth Supplemental Indenture is set forth under Item 8.01 of the Current Report on Form 8-K filed by the Company on March 22, 2017 in connection with the closing of the offering of the Notes and is
incorporated by reference into this Item 8.01. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The legal opinion as to the legality of the Option Notes sold is being filed as Exhibit
5.1 to this Current Report on Form <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">8-K</FONT> and is incorporated herein and into the Registration Statement by reference. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><I>Additional Note Hedge Transactions </I></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On
March&nbsp;27, 2017, in connection with the exercise by the Underwriters of their option to purchase the Option Notes, the Company entered into additional note hedge transactions with each of Goldman, Sachs&nbsp;&amp; Co., Deutsche Bank Securities
Inc., Citigroup Global Markets Inc., Morgan Stanley&nbsp;&amp; Co. LLC, Barclays Capital Inc. or their respective affiliates (the &#147;<U>Hedge Counterparties</U>&#148;), pursuant to call option confirmations in substantially the form of Exhibit
10.1 to this Current Report on Form <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">8-K,</FONT> which is incorporated herein by reference. The additional note hedge transactions are expected generally to reduce the potential dilution to the common stock of the
Company, par value $0.001 per share (the &#147;<U>Common Stock</U>&#148;), and/or offset potential cash payments in excess of the principal amount upon any conversion of the Option Notes in the event that the market value per share of the Common
Stock, as measured under the terms of the additional note hedge transactions, is greater than the strike prices of the additional note hedge transactions (which correspond to the initial conversion price of the Notes and are subject to certain
adjustments substantially similar to those contained in the Notes). On March&nbsp;30, 2017, at the closing of the offering of the Option Notes, the Company paid an aggregate amount of approximately $26.6&nbsp;million to the Hedge Counterparties for
the additional note hedge transactions. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><I>Additional Warrant Transactions </I></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On March&nbsp;27, 2017, in connection with the exercise by the Underwriters of their option to purchase the Option Notes, the Company entered
into additional warrant confirmations with the Hedge Counterparties in substantially the form of Exhibit 10.2 to this Current Report on Form <FONT STYLE="white-space:nowrap">8-K,</FONT> which is incorporated herein by reference, pursuant to which
the Company issued certain warrants (the &#147;<U>Additional Warrants</U>&#148;). The Additional Warrants allow the Hedge Counterparties to acquire, subject to anti-dilution adjustments, up to approximately 0.8&nbsp;million shares of Common Stock at
a strike price of $655.00 per share in respect of Additional Warrants relating to the Option Notes, which is also subject to adjustment. The Additional Warrants would separately have a dilutive effect to the extent that the market value per share of
the Common Stock exceeds the strike price of the Additional Warrants </P>
