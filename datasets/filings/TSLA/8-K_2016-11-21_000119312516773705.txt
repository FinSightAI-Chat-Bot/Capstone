 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Introductory Note </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:8%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On November 21, 2016 (the &#147;<U>Closing Date</U>&#148;), upon the terms and subject to the conditions set forth in the Agreement and Plan of
Merger (the &#147;<U>Merger Agreement</U>&#148;), dated as of July 31, 2016, among Tesla Motors, Inc. (&#147;<U>Tesla</U>&#148;), SolarCity Corporation (&#147;<U>SolarCity</U>&#148;) and D Subsidiary, Inc., a wholly owned subsidiary of Tesla
(&#147;<U>Merger Sub</U>&#148;), and in accordance with applicable law, Merger Sub merged with and into SolarCity (the &#147;<U>Merger</U>&#148;), with SolarCity surviving the Merger as a wholly owned subsidiary of Tesla. </P>
<P STYLE="font-size:18pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>
<TABLE STYLE="BORDER-COLLAPSE:COLLAPSE; font-family:Times New Roman; font-size:10pt" BORDER="0" CELLPADDING="0" CELLSPACING="0" WIDTH="100%">

<TD WIDTH="12%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left"><B>Item&nbsp;1.01.</B></TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><B>Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement. </B></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:8%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In connection with the completion of the
Merger, SolarCity entered into supplemental indentures (collectively, the &#147;<U>Supplemental Indentures</U>&#148;) with respect to each of (i) the Indenture, dated as of October 21, 2013, between SolarCity and Wells Fargo Bank, National
Association, as trustee, governing SolarCity&#146;s 2.75% Convertible Senior Notes due 2018, (ii) the Indenture, dated as of September 30, 2014, between SolarCity and Wells Fargo Bank, National Association, as trustee, governing SolarCity&#146;s
1.625% Convertible Senior Notes due 2019, and (iii) the Indenture, dated as of December 7, 2015, between SolarCity and Wells Fargo Bank, National Association, as trustee, governing SolarCity&#146;s Zero Coupon Convertible Senior Notes due 2020. The
Supplemental Indentures provide that, following the consummation of the Merger, the holders of SolarCity&#146;s convertible notes will have the right to convert each $1,000 of principal amount of their convertible notes into, in lieu of SolarCity
Common Stock (as defined below), the number of shares of Tesla Common Stock (as defined below) that a holder of a number of shares of SolarCity Common Stock equal to the conversion rate immediately prior to the effective time of the Merger would
have been entitled to receive upon consummation of the Merger. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:8%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The foregoing description of the Supplemental Indentures does not purport
to be complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference to the Supplemental Indentures, which are included as Exhibits 4.1, 4.2 and 4.3 hereto and incorporated into this Item 1.01 by reference. </P>
<P STYLE="font-size:18pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>

<TR>
<TD WIDTH="12%" VALIGN="top" ALIGN="left"><B>Item&nbsp;2.01.</B></TD>
<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><B>Completion of Acquisition or Disposition of Assets. </B></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:8%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">As described above, on the Closing
Date, Tesla completed its previously announced acquisition of SolarCity. As a result of the Merger, SolarCity became a wholly owned subsidiary of Tesla. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:8%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Under the terms of the Merger Agreement, at the effective time of the Merger (the &#147;<U>Effective Time</U>&#148;), each share of SolarCity
common stock, par value $0.0001 per share (the &#147;<U>SolarCity Common Stock</U>&#148;), issued and outstanding immediately prior to the Effective Time (other than shares of SolarCity Common Stock owned by SolarCity as treasury stock or owned by
Tesla or Merger Sub, which were cancelled) was converted into the right to receive 0.110 (the &#147;<U>Exchange Ratio</U>&#148;) shares of Tesla common stock, par value $0.001 per share (the &#147;<U>Tesla Common Stock</U>&#148;). No fractional
shares of Tesla Common Stock were issued in the Merger, and SolarCity stockholders became entitled to receive cash in lieu of any fractional shares. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:8%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">At the Effective Time, SolarCity options and SolarCity restricted stock unit awards were converted into corresponding equity awards in respect
of Tesla Common Stock based on the Exchange Ratio, with the awards retaining the same vesting and other terms and conditions as in effect immediately prior to consummation of the Merger (except for certain founder options granted in 2015, which were
cancelled for no consideration). </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:8%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Upon the closing of the Merger, the shares of SolarCity Common Stock, which previously traded under the
ticker symbol &#147;SCTY&#148; on the NASDAQ Stock Market (the &#147;<U>NASDAQ</U>&#148;), have ceased trading on, and were delisted from, the NASDAQ. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:8%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The foregoing description of the Merger and the Merger Agreement does not purport to be complete and is qualified in its entirety by reference
to the Merger Agreement, a copy of which was attached as Exhibit 2.1 to Tesla&#146;s Current Report on Form 8-K filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the &#147;<U>SEC</U>&#148;) on August 1, 2016, and is incorporated herein by
reference. </P>
